The motivating inspirators that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Outrageously courageous - they disturb the status quo and define a new normal
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader
  • Big footprint - impact on millions and change the world
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Victoria is a former D1 volleyball player, TED talk speaker, mental health and body image advocate, who is deeply passionate about supporting and inspiring young women. She was a four-year starter for USC, who finished 1st in total digs for the Pac-12 conference and finished in the top 5 most digs in USC history. Not only was she known for her breaking records, but she began publicly advocating about mental health during her sophomore year. Through her work advocating for mental health, she has inspired and motivated those around her to become their best selves.
